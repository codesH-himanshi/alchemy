Select a conversion type from the navigation menu.

Temperature



To convert celsuis to fahrenheit yourself, use this formula replacing the `C` with your temperature in celsuis: (C × 9/5) + 32
Weight



To convert pounds to kilograms yourself, use this formula replacing the `P` with your weight in pounds: P / 2.2046
Distance



To convert miles to kilometers yourself, use this formula replacing the `M` with your distance in miles: M * 1.609344
